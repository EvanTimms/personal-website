About Me

Let me tell you a few things...

BIO:

I am a Computer Engineering student from the University of Alberta. Ever since I was young I have been fascinated with the way technology works and how it can be used to make our world a better place. From building robotic arms to designing and building web applications, I have experienced a huge variety of different types of software development.

I am mainly focused on developing web applications currently, but plan to move into mobile development in the near future.

Outside of school I am super into photography, music and art. I love rock climbing and mountian biking in the summer and skiing in the winter. Check out my blog for some more interests of mine!
